__Demo Script__

I built an AI art mentor that works kind of like a real creative coach you would learn from one on one.

People upload their sketches, and the system looks at things like anatomy, shading, perspective, and composition, then gives structured feedback that feels like an actual art teacher reviewing your work.

What makes it more interesting is that it does not just give a one time critique. It actually tracks your improvement over time and builds a progress profile so you can see how you are growing.

It is a full stack system. The frontend is built in React, the backend uses FastAPI, OpenAI handles the evaluation, and Supabase manages login and stores user history so everything is saved.

The design is intentionally very clean and modern, like a real SaaS product you would pay for. It has that Stripe or Notion style feel with soft glass like UI, spacing focused layout, and a clear flow instead of clutter.

Overall the idea was not just to make an AI tool that gives feedback, but to simulate a real product experience with progress, retention, and identity built in.
